Member Biographies

Harvey Dale

Lead singer Harvey Dale has been obsessed with performing since a young age. From participating in school band performances and plays to dancing and being an on-stage backup for several artists, Harvey is ready to begin his own artist journey and create the music he believes the scene needs.

Barbara Cement

Barbara is a talented guitarist with more than 20 years of experience in the Austin music scene, performing duo as "The Cements" with her brother Larry. Since joining Harvey with her brother to create Harvey Dale and the Cements, Barbara looks forward to touring and greeting fans.

Larry Cement

Drummer Larry Cement, the other half of the Cements, met Harvey more than 15 years ago when they roomed together while pursuing their undergraduate degrees at the University of Texas at Austin. Since meeting, the two have been unseparable, and Larry says he's fortunate enough to tour with his best friend and sister.
